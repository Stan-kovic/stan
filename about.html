ABOUT US

Stankovic is a business established in 2018. It started as a pig farm and our target as at this time was to meet the huge demand in this part of the world where there is a minority of people practicing religions that forbid consumption of pork meat.

In the year 2020, we ventured into Information and communication technology such as visual communication (graphics design - to solve problems through the use of typography, photography, iconography and illustrations), solar energy (to cure the epidermic in business, homes, etc caused by the poor power supply from the national grid in this part of the world) and Security System (CCTV surveillance for security and as an investigative tool).

We at Stankovic are moving with the current trend to make sure our customers get the full utilization of these services. Also, we offer opportunities for those who wish to be professionals in some of these areas we specialize with compehensive training and tutorials.

Our Mission

Our mission is to set yardsticks of virtue, improve in our quality, accelerate our capabilities, expand our efficiency in delivering high quality technology solutions to our clients, and help their businesses grow through graphics and tutorials to improve their skills.

Opportunities

Our sole aim is to ensure that our clients are satisfied with the services rendered and as well, engage in research to find ways to improve these services.
